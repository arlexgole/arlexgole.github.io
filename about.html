My name is Arlex Gole and I am currently a junior majoring in HCI and Business at CMU. On campus, I am the co-president of Global Business Brigades (a movement for building economic opportunities in the developing world) and member on the club basketball team (#ballislife).

I was born in Kathmandhu, Nepal but moved to New York City when I about 9 years old. Having lived in such different places and experiencing how technology can drastically impact everyday life, I aspire to design products that help make life easier.
